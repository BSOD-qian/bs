Unit 1笔记

发表于 2022-09-05 更新于 2022-09-13 分类于 内容
  1. historical [his’t?rik?l] adj. 历史的，历史题材的
    　historical interests
  2. Greek 希腊人,希腊语 n. 希腊的 adj.
    　Greece 希腊 n.
  3. Egypt [‘i:d??pt] 埃及 Egyptian [?’d??p??n] 埃及的, 埃及人
    　Pyramids are tombs built for the kings and queens in Egypt.
  4. imagination [i,m?d?i’nei??n] 想象力 n.
    　The story shows plenty of imagination
    imagine [i’m?d?in] v. 想象
    　He imagined himself as manager of the company.
  5. at a time 每一次
    　Take the pills two at a time.
  6. no longer 不再：not any longer
    　After he said sorry to her, she no longer complained.
  7. Troy 特洛伊 [tr?i] (小亚西亚的古城)
    　the Trojans [‘tr?ud??n] 特洛伊人
  8. beyond prep. 在…较远的一边
    　What lies beyond the mountains?
  9. Outside the main gates of the city stood a huge wooden horse. (倒装句）
    　A huge wooden horse stood outside the main gates of the city. (表示强调或引起注意)
  10. interrupt v. [,?nt?’r?pt] 打断
    　Don’t interrupt him when he is making a speech.
    interruption n. [,?nt?’r?p??n] 打断, 障碍物
  11. obey v. 服从，顺从
    　Soldiers have to obey rules all the time.
    　disobey v. 不服从
    　He?was?punished?for?disobeying?orders.? 他因违抗命令而受到惩罚。
  12. drag: pull roughly 拖,拉 dragged–dragged
    　The car hit the man and dragged him along the road.
  13. citizen [‘sitiz?n] n. 公民，市民
    　It is the duty of every citizen to keep the environment clean.
  14. celebrate v. 庆祝
    celebration n. 庆祝,祝贺
  15. make jokes about sb. 调侃某人
    　The poor boy is very ugly. His classmates always make jokes about his appearance.
    　　play a joke/trick on sb. 戏弄某人
  16. make sure 确保
    Make sure that you have fastened your seat belt.
  17. secure [si’kju?] a. 安全的 securely adv.安全地 : safely
    They have arrived there securely.
  18. including prep. 包括….在内
    　　The band played many songs, including some of my favourites.
    　　include [in’klu:d] v. 包括,包含
    　　The tour included a visit to the Science Museum.
  19. except for 除…之外
    except for 后接的词同句子的主语一般不是同类的,指从整体中除去一个细节,一个方面
    　　Your composition is good except for a few grammar mistakes.
    　　There was silence, except for the singing of birds.
    　　except 除去,不包括
    　　后接的词同主语一般是同类的,指在同类的整体中除去一个部分
    They all went there except Li Ming.
    She saw nothing except snow.
    besides 除了…之外又加上 …
    Besides Li Ming, there are many other students attending the meeting.
  20. secret [‘si:krit] n. 秘密 adj. 秘密的
    The plan is secret.
    She?was?secretly?pleased?to?see?him.? 见到他，她心中窃喜。
  21. darkness n. [dɑ:kn?s] 黑暗;阴暗
    After a few minutes our eyes got used to the darkness.
  22. seize v. [si:z] 抓, 捉
    The policeman seized the thief.
  23. capture v. [‘k?pt??] 俘获,攻占
    　The police failed to capture the robbers.
    captive n. [‘k?ptiv] 俘虏
  24. succeed in sth./doing sth.
    People have succeeded in putting astronauts on the moon.
    be successful in
  25. mend [mend] 修理,修补
    Can you mend the hole in my shirt?
  26. get rid of 处理掉, 丢弃，摆脱
    Try to get rid of the flies in the kitchen.
    How can we get rid of these unwelcome visitors?
