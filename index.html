I'm not nervous any longer

发表于 2022-09-13 分类于 内容

My life has changed a lot in the past few years. I used to be shy and nervous. Once，I took part in a piano competition and I was so nervous that I made a mistake on the stage. As you can imagine，I was so embarrassed at that moment. As a result，I failed in the competition and lost the opportunity of being better. Therefore，I determined to overcome this weak point by taking part in more activities so that my confidence got enhanced through practices and experiences. Now，I’m more outgoing than I used to be .The biggest change in my life is that I’m not nervous any longer and I’ve succeeded in overcoming my weak point.
